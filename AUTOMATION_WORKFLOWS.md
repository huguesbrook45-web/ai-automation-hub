# Automation Workflows & AI Prompts

## Weekly Content Generation Workflow

### Prompt 1: Generate Solution Guide

```
You are an expert automation consultant writing for solopreneurs and small business owners.

Write a comprehensive, proof-based guide (800-1200 words) on: [TOPIC]

Structure:
1. Problem Statement (100 words) - Why this matters
2. Solution Overview (150 words) - What you'll learn
3. Step-by-Step Implementation (400 words) - Detailed walkthrough
4. Real Workflow Example (150 words) - Screenshot/description of actual use
5. Tools You'll Need (100 words) - Specific recommendations with affiliate links
6. Common Mistakes (100 words) - What to avoid
7. Next Steps (100 words) - How to scale

Tone: Friendly, practical, no hype. Focus on clarity and immediate utility.
Include specific numbers, timelines, and cost estimates where relevant.
```

### Prompt 2: Generate Email Sequence

```
Create 2 short, actionable emails for solopreneurs:

Email 1 (Tip): Share a specific automation tip that saves 2+ hours/week
- Subject line (compelling, not clickbait)
- Hook (1 sentence problem)
- Solution (3-4 sentences, specific)
- CTA (link to relevant guide or tool)

Email 2 (Recommendation): Recommend a specific tool with proof
- Subject line (curiosity-driven)
- Problem it solves
- How it works (1 paragraph)
- Why I recommend it (specific use case)
- Affiliate link with tracking parameter

Tone: Conversational, helpful, genuine recommendations only.
```

### Prompt 3: Generate Social Media Posts

```
Create 3 social media posts for LinkedIn and Twitter:

Post 1: Share a surprising automation stat
- Hook: Surprising fact or question
- Context: Why it matters
- CTA: Link to relevant guide

Post 2: Share a quick automation tip
- Problem: What's the pain point?
- Solution: 1-sentence fix
- Proof: Specific result or metric
- CTA: Link to full guide

Post 3: Share a success story or case study
- Situation: What was the challenge?
- Action: What automation was implemented?
- Result: Specific metrics and outcomes
- CTA: Link to blueprint or guide

Format for Twitter: Keep under 280 characters
Format for LinkedIn: 150-300 characters (encourage engagement)
```

## Performance Monitoring

### Weekly Metrics to Track

- **Traffic:** Total visitors, new visitors, returning visitors
- **Engagement:** Time on page, scroll depth, click-through rate
- **Conversions:** Email signups, calculator uses, guide downloads
- **Affiliate:** Link clicks, conversions, revenue

### Monthly Analysis

1. **Top Performing Content:** Which guides get the most traffic?
2. **Conversion Funnels:** Where do visitors drop off?
3. **Affiliate Performance:** Which tools convert best?
4. **Email Metrics:** Open rates, click rates, unsubscribe rates

### Optimization Actions

- **Low Traffic Guides:** Update with better keywords, add to email sequence
- **High Traffic, Low Conversions:** Add clearer CTAs, improve affiliate placement
- **Low Email Open Rates:** Test new subject lines
- **Low Product Sales:** Offer discount, improve product page
